How to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email to privacy@jidaba.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered phone number in the email
  3. We will send a verification code to your phone
  4. Reply to our email with the verification code
  5. Your account will be deleted within 48 hours of verification

Data Deletion Information

Data that will be permanently deleted:

  • Personal Information (name, phone number, email)
  • Transaction History
  • Account Settings and Preferences
  • All Associated Services and Data

Data Retention Period:

  • Account data: Deleted within 48 hours of verified request
  • Backup data: Deleted within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained for up to 90 days as required by law
⚠️ Important Notice:
  • This process cannot be undone
  • All account data will be permanently deleted
  • You will lose access to all services
  • Any pending transactions must be completed before deletion

Need Help?

If you need assistance with account deletion or have any questions, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@jidaba.app
  • Support Hours: 24/7
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
