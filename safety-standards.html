Jidaba Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Safety

At Jidaba, we are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all our users. We have zero tolerance for any form of abuse, exploitation, or inappropriate behavior, especially concerning minors.

Prevention Measures

  • Strict user verification through phone number and official ID
  • Active monitoring of user interactions and transactions
  • Immediate action on any reported violations
  • Regular safety audits and updates
  • Collaboration with relevant authorities

Reporting Concerns

If you notice any suspicious activity or have concerns about user safety:

  • Use the in-app reporting feature
  • Email us at safety@jidaba.app
  • Contact local authorities for immediate threats

Our Response Process

  1. Immediate acknowledgment of reports (within 24 hours)
  2. Investigation of reported incidents
  3. Account suspension when necessary
  4. Cooperation with law enforcement
  5. Follow-up with reporting users

Contact Information

For safety-related concerns:

  • Email: safety@jidaba.app
  • Emergency Contact: web.oubaha@gmail.com
  • Available: 24/7 for urgent matters

Legal Compliance

Jidaba complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations. We actively cooperate with regional and national authorities in preventing and reporting any form of abuse or exploitation.
